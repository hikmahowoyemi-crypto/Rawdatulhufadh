About Us

Where the Quran Meets the Heart

Rawdatul Hufadh — رَوْضَةُ الحُفَّاظ — is a madrasah dedicated to the preservation, understanding, and embodiment of the Noble Quran. Rooted in authentic Islamic scholarship, we provide a structured, nurturing environment for students of all ages.

Our experienced Ustadz guide every student through a comprehensive Quranic curriculum — from foundational recitation to complete memorisation — always with patience, care, and taqwā.

Class Schedule

Monday – Saturday  |  9:00 AM – 2:00 PM

Consistent, structured daily learning for optimal Quran retention.

Our Foundation

Vision & Mission

👁

Our Vision

رُؤْيَتُنَا

أَنْ نَكُونَ مَرْجِعًا رَائِدًا فِي تَحْفِيظِ الْقُرْآنِ الْكَرِيمِ وَتَعْلِيمِ الْعُلُومِ الإِسْلَامِيَّةِ، مُرَبِّينَ جِيلاً مُسْتَنِيرًا بِنُورِ الْوَحْيِ.

To be a leading centre of Quranic memorisation and Islamic sciences, raising a generation illuminated by the light of divine revelation — carrying the Quran in their hearts and living it in their character.

🌱

Our Mission

مُهِمَّتُنَا

تَقْدِيمُ تَعْلِيمٍ قُرْآنِيٍّ أَصِيلٍ فِي بِيئَةٍ مُحِبَّةٍ، تَجْمَعُ بَيْنَ الحِفْظِ وَالتَّجْوِيدِ وَالتَّرْبِيَةِ الإِسْلَامِيَّةِ الشَّامِلَةِ.

To provide authentic Quranic education in a loving environment, combining Hifz, Tajweed, Arabic language, and wholesome Islamic character development under qualified and devoted teachers.

What We Offer

Our Programs & Courses

📖

Hifz ul-Quran

حِفْظُ الْقُرْآنِ الْكَرِيمِ

Complete memorisation of the Noble Quran under qualified Huffadh, with consistent revision and structured testing.

🎙

Tajweed

أَحْكَامُ التَّجْوِيدِ

Mastering the rules of Quranic recitation — Makhaarij, Sifaat, and the modes of recitation — for beautiful, accurate tilāwah.

✍

Qa'idah & Noorani

الْقَاعِدَةُ النُّورَانِيَّةُ

Beginner-level Quran reading for young students, building a solid foundation in Arabic letters and pronunciation.

🌙

Islamic Studies

الدِّرَاسَاتُ الإِسْلَامِيَّةُ

Fiqh, Sīrah, Aqeedah, and Hadith studies to build complete understanding of the Deen alongside the Quran.

🔤

Arabic Language

تَعْلِيمُ اللُّغَةِ الْعَرَبِيَّةِ

Arabic grammar (Nahw & Sarf) and vocabulary to help students understand and connect deeply with the Quran.

🔁

Murāja'ah & Revision

الْمُرَاجَعَةُ وَالتَّثْبِيتُ

Structured daily revision sessions ensuring long-term retention of all memorised Quranic portions.
